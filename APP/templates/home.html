ExamEase

is a management system designed to ease the exam evaluation process. Built using Python’s Django framework with Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) technology integrated within the system, ExamEase allows users to check exam sheets with a single click, automating the grading process and reducing manual effort. ExamEase also allows users to create their examination, input their answer keys, add students, and administer exams directly within the system. ExamEase – Your OMR solution!
